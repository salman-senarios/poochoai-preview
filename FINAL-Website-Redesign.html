Frequently Asked Questions

What languages does Poocho AI support?
Poocho AI natively supports Urdu, Arabic, English, Bangla, and 100+ languages. Our models are trained on regional dialects and cultural context — not just translated.
Can I deploy on my own infrastructure?
Yes. Poocho AI supports on-premise, private cloud, and hybrid deployments. Fully compliant with data localization laws in Pakistan, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and across the MENA region.
How long does implementation take?
Most deployments go live within 48 hours. Complex enterprise integrations typically take 1-2 weeks with our dedicated implementation team.
Is my data secure?
Your data never leaves your environment in on-premise deployments. We're ISO 27001 aligned with full audit trails, encryption at rest and in transit, and role-based access.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card required to start.

Poocho AI integrates with major CRMs (Salesforce, Dynamics), telephony (Asterisk, FreeSWITCH), and messaging platforms (WhatsApp Business API, Facebook). Custom APIs available.
