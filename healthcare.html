Appointment booking & rescheduling

Book by specialty, consultant, or slot preference. Handles rescheduling and cancellations. Reduces no-show rate with smart reminders in the patient's language.

Post-discharge follow-up

Outbound calls after discharge to check recovery, medication adherence, and red-flag symptoms — with clinical escalation when needed.

Lab-result delivery

Securely deliver routine lab results with plain-language explanation. Abnormal results escalate to the ordering physician with patient context pre-loaded.

Triage & urgent-care routing

Symptom triage using your clinical protocols. Routes to telemedicine, OPD booking, ED, or immediate emergency services — with clinician oversight of thresholds.

Prescription refill requests

Accept refill requests, verify patient identity, check prescription validity, and route to the pharmacist or physician per your refill policy.

Multi-language patient support

Native Urdu, Arabic, and English — including low-literacy phrasing and regional dialects. Patients don't have to switch language mid-call.
