Frequently Asked Questions

What languages does Poocho AI support?
Poocho AI natively supports Urdu, Arabic, English, Bangla, and 100+ languages. Our models are trained on regional dialects and cultural context — not just translated.
Can I deploy on my own infrastructure?
Yes. Poocho AI supports on-premise, private cloud, and hybrid deployments. The platform is architected to support data-residency requirements in Pakistan, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and across the MENA region — customers typically complete a regulator-facing compliance review during onboarding.
How long does implementation take?
Most deployments go live within 48 hours. Complex enterprise integrations typically take 1-2 weeks with our dedicated implementation team.
Is my data secure?
Your data never leaves your environment in on-premise deployments. Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3), role-based access controls, and full tamper-evident audit trails are built in. Security posture and available certifications are shared under NDA during procurement.
Do you offer a free trial?
Pilot programs are available for qualified enterprise customers after a brief scoping call. We'll set you up with a sandbox environment and sample workloads for your industry.
Can Poocho AI integrate with our existing systems?
Poocho AI integrates with major CRMs (Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics), core-banking platforms (Finacle, T24, Flexcube), telephony stacks (Asterisk, FreeSWITCH, Avaya, Cisco UCM), and messaging platforms (WhatsApp Business API, Facebook). Custom APIs and pre-built ESB adapters available. For customers without a modern voice stack, we can deliver the underlying telephony layer end-to-end — single vendor, single SLA.
