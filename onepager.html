The problem

Banks, telcos, governments, and healthcare providers across Pakistan, the GCC, and South Asia handle millions of customer calls a year — in Urdu, Arabic, Punjabi, Sindhi, and regional dialects.

Global conversational-AI platforms "support" these languages via translation layers that fail at scale. Local BPO labour is unsustainably expensive at peak. Data-residency laws (PDPL, DPL UAE, KSA PDPL) rule out most SaaS options.

The solution

Poocho AI is voice and chat AI trained natively in Urdu, Arabic, and English — including regional dialects and code-switching. Deployed on the customer's own servers. One AI brain across voice, WhatsApp, and web chat.

Optional managed telephony layer (SIP, PBX, recording) for organisations that want a single-vendor, single-SLA voice stack end-to-end.

Why now

  • Native-language LLMs finally crossed the quality threshold for regulated production work.
  • Data-residency laws in PK, UAE, KSA, EU make on-premise deployment a procurement requirement, not a preference.
  • Contact-centre wage inflation + customer expectations make AI no longer optional for regional ops teams.

Product

  • Voice + chat + WhatsApp agents in Urdu, Arabic, English, and 100+ languages.
  • Custom TTS, ASR, and LLMs tuned for regional dialects.
