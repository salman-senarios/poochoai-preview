Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on poochoai.com, and how you can manage your preferences.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (such as consent choices) and provide analytics to improve our content.

Categories of cookies we use

Strictly necessary

Required for the website to function. These include cookies that store your consent choice. You cannot opt out of these.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate visitor behavior (pages visited, time on site, traffic source). IP addresses are anonymized. These cookies are only set if you accept analytics cookies via the banner.

  • _ga, _ga_* — Google Analytics identifiers (2-year expiry)

Third-party embeds

When you interact with the demo form, Formspree may set cookies required to deliver your message. Formspree's cookie usage is governed by their own policy.

Managing your preferences

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies using the banner that appears on your first visit. To change your choice later, clear your browser's site data for poochoai.com and reload the page — the banner will reappear.

Most browsers also allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Note that blocking strictly-necessary cookies may break parts of the site.

Do Not Track

We honor "Do Not Track" browser signals for analytics cookies. If your browser sends DNT, we will not load analytics unless you explicitly accept.

Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change.
